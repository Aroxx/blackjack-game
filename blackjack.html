Blackjack Game - version 1.1

    Let's play Blackjack!

    Rules:

    • Score higher than the dealer while getting as close to 21 as you can without going over.
    • Cards are counted as the value of the card number, face cards all count as 10, Aces can count as 1 or 11.
    • Enter a bet amount and click "Place Bet" before you deal a new hand.
    • Click "Deal Hand" to start, then click "Hit" to be dealt a new card and "Stand" to stay where you are. The dealer will then play their hand.
